Privacy Policy for Trackord

At Trackord, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Trackord and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

Collection of personal information

By default, we do not collect any personal data automatically within the app. In the event of you contacting us for request or support, it is required that you provide information such as your name, email address, phone number, the contents of the message and/or attachments you may send us, and any other information you may choose to provide. By doing so you indicate that you have read this Privacy Policy and agree with its contents.

Sharing of personal information

Any personal information collected will not be shared with any other organisation or third party unless absolutely required for development works to be carried out. Any sharing of information will not be carried out without explicitly asking for permission. Any other party will be bound by confidentiality and privacy agreements to ensure the continued protection of the personal information.

Use of personal information

    Your personal information may be used for the following activities:
  • Communicating with you such as responding to your emails for better supporting you
  • Internal records

    • Application Data

    Personal data stored within the Trackord application is stored locally on each user’s device. This includes all records and settings. Trackord data is private and not accessible via any other means than the available “Export to CSV” option within the app, at user's own discretion.

    Changes to this policy

    This Privacy Policy may be changed in the future, and should be checked to ensure your agreement to its terms. If you have objections to such changes, you should not access or use this app. Your continued use of the app indicates that you accept these changes.

    Contacting Us

    If you have any concerns about this Privacy Policy, want to ask any questions or would like to discuss anything about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via email at trackord.dev@gmail.com, and we will respond as soon as reasonably possible.
